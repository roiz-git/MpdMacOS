# Maestro - MPD Client Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Maestro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Storage

- Server profiles are stored locally on your device only
- Playlist cache is stored locally for search performance
- No data is sent to external servers or third parties

## Network Connections

- The app only connects to MPD servers you configure
- No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting
- No advertising or tracking

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, https://github.com/roiz-git/MpdMacOS/issues
